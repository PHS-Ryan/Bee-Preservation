What is Kaitiakitanga?

Kaitiakitanga is a Māori concept from New Zealand that means guardianship, especially of the environment. It involves taking responsibility to protect and care for the natural world, water, plants, and animals so they can be sustained for future generations. It’s about respect, balance, and maintaining the health of ecosystems through management and connection to the environment.
